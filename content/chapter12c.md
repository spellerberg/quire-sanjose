## Guadalupana Officers year-by-year
Sociedad de Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe

Officers of La Sociedad de Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe at San Jose Church were almost always nominated at the November meeting, elected at the December meeting and installed at the January meeting. Celadores and minor officers were often announced in February.

The following list of officers was taken from the Sunday Bulletins of 1960 until 1996 and from internal documents of the Society after 1996. Officers are listed by year of service.

Pete Botello of the San José Post was elected State Commander of the Catholic War Veterans in May 1961 (58) (223:05/07/61). This was a significant honor.
